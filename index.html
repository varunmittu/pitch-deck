Strategy

Three Revenue Streams. One Brand. Compounding Growth.

Direct Sales

Live Now

  • E-commerce: Amazon, JioMart, Shopsy, Flipkart, Myntra, Nykaa
  • Quick commerce: Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart, BigBasket
  • General Trade: D-Mart, supermarkets, Lulu Mall
  • Margin: 15–20% online | Variable offline

Fluno Licensing Model

Months 4–8 Post-Funding

  • Online License: Authorized resellers list on any e-commerce platform.
  • Offline License: Territory distributor + retailer licenses with 10+ year brand agreements.
  • Revenue: License fee + % of partner's sales

B2B Bulk Supply

Months 8–12+ Post-Funding

  • Direct bulk supply to D-Mart, national supermarket chains, Lulu Mall.
  • High volume, predictable, recurring revenue.
  • Revenue: Bulk supply margin at scale

Most brands grow linearly. Fluno is built to grow exponentially — every new licensee, distributor, and B2B partner multiplies revenue without proportional capital requirement.
